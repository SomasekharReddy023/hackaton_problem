Title: Essential Soft Skills for Career Success

**Core Soft Skills:**
1. **Communication:** Expressing ideas clearly, listening actively.
2. **Teamwork:** Collaborating effectively with others.
3. **Problem-Solving:** Analyzing issues and proposing solutions.
4. **Adaptability:** Thriving in dynamic environments.
5. **Time Management:** Prioritizing tasks and meeting deadlines.
6. **Emotional Intelligence:** Understanding and managing emotions.

**Tips to Improve:**
- Join group projects to strengthen collaboration.
- Practice public speaking.
- Take feedback constructively.
- Stay curious and open-minded.
