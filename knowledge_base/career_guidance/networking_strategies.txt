Title: Effective Networking Strategies

**Why Networking Matters:**
Networking helps professionals discover opportunities, gain mentorship, and build meaningful relationships that support career growth.

**Best Practices:**
- **Leverage LinkedIn:** Connect with peers, recruiters, and industry leaders.
- **Attend Events:** Join conferences, webinars, and career fairs.
- **Engage in Communities:** Participate in online forums, GitHub, or Slack groups.
- **Offer Value First:** Share knowledge, resources, or introductions.
- **Follow Up:** Maintain connections by staying in touch periodically.

**Golden Rule:**
Networking is about building relationships, not just asking for favors.
